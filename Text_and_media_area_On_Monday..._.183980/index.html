On Wednesday, Jan.28, we will first consider the Hellenistic art, the last period in the art of Ancient Greece.   

Then we'll turn to the Greek Architecture and architectural sculpture of Ancient Greece. Read the Development of Greek architecture and architectural sculpture in Adam’s Art across Time, vol. 1, pages 159—177 (the PDF file is available in the previous week's section). The purpose is to understand how art is represented in Greek architecture. The images for discussion are given below.

Ancient Greece influenced the architecture of countries far from it. Find on the Internet examples of Greek-style architecture in the United States, Russia, and Central Asia (in Kyrgyzstan or Tajikistan), and submit three images in the forum opened in this section.
