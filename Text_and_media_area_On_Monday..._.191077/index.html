Description

On Monday,  Feb 16, we will first complete the High Renaissance (Michelangelo's and Raphael's art), and then we will move to the consideration of the Venetian School. Read Adams' Art across Time for the Venetian School, p. 573-585. We'll focus primarily on the art of Giorgione and Titian. Also read the inserts: The Dispute over Painting vs Sculpture p. 578 and Pietro Aretino and Color vs Drawing p. 583.

On Wednesday, Feb 18, we'll take Mannerism and later 16th-century painting in Italy (Tintoretto),, thus concluding the topic of the European Renaissance.  The assigned reading for Mannerism is from Adams' Art across Time:

Mannerism p. 587-588;  Tintoretto p. 598;  El Greco p.599.

Also, read the following inserts (boxes): The Reformation (p. 588); The Counter-Reformation (p. 591). These boxes deal with the political and social events that had an impact on Renaissance art.

Just to remind you: you have the digital copy of the full text of Art across Time  uploaded  in the previous section and also at the top  of the e-course/
